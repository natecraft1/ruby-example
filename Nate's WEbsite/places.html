I've been places, but not enough. Spending a semester in Italy was the best decision of my life because it introduced me to the wonders of travel. I've since lived abroad for over 3 years. 2 of them being in Ljubljana, Slovenia. I am incredibly nostalgic of that place and the friends I left behind. But at least I took something very important: the knowledge that he who lives sees, but he who travels sees more, and I hope I will always continue to see more than I've seen thus far.
